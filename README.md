Flipkart Sales Dashboard 
--Overview

This project presents an interactive Excel dashboard developed to analyze Flipkart sales data. It focuses on tracking key performance indicators (KPIs), identifying trends, and delivering actionable insights to support data-driven decision-making.

--Dataset

The dataset includes key e-commerce attributes such as:

Order details: order_id, order_date
Product data: product_id, product_category
Pricing: price, discount_percent, discounted_price
Sales metrics: quantity_sold, revenue
Customer info: customer_region, payment_method
Feedback: rating, review_count
Time: month, year

--Dashboard Features

KPIs
Total Orders
Total Quantity Sold
Average Price
Total Revenue
Average Order Value (AOV)

--Visualizations

Monthly Revenue & Orders Trend
Order Distribution by Category
Sales Forecast
Total Sales by Category
Top 5 Products by Quantity
Orders by Payment Method

-- Interactivity
Year Filter
Month Filter
Payment Method Filter
Timeline (Date Range)

--Key Insights
Seasonal patterns observed in sales performance
High-performing categories contribute majority revenue
Digital payment methods dominate transactions
Forecasting supports future planning
